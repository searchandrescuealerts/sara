About SARA

SARA (Search and Rescue Alerts) engages the power of the public to help bring kids home. When someone goes missing, the first moments are the most critical. SARA immediately sends a text to your mobile device so you can keep an eye out as you go about your daily activities. Messages include a photo and description of the individuals, current information, and details about organized searches. You become part of a vast community of concerned searchers with eyes on the ground and everywhere. You can help us bring them swiftly and safely home.

“I would want everyone looking for my child if something went wrong.”

To receive notifications of missing persons in your area, register to be a SARA Citizen today.There is no charge to register.

How You Can Help

You never know when you will see something that makes the difference. Just being alert on the roads, at the store, and in your own neighborhood will help.

Text messages, with a link to a photo and contact information, are sent to your mobile device from approved Law Enforcement Agencies.

Start helping with a search from right where you are.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I place more than one point on the map?
At this time we do not accept more than one location, but we will include that in a future version.

How many messages will I receive?
This is entirely dependent on your local law enforcement agency. If you live in an area where many people go missing, you may receive lots. But you if you feel like you are receiving too many alerts you are welcome to log back in and make your location area smaller.

Is SARA a government agency?
No. SARA is a private company founded by concerned citizens who wish to help find missing persons. SARA does work closely with and provide support to local and national government agencies, police departments, cities, and other private organizations.
