About

SARA (Search and Rescue Alerts) harnesses the power of caring citizens to help find missing people and return them safely home. SARA uses cell phone texts to alert the public about missing persons so they can help search as they go about their daily activities. Text messages are sent to your phone from approved Law Enforcement agencies which may include a photo and description of the individual, information about the circumstances, and details about organized searches or places where tips can be directed. A Missing Person Poster in the palm of your hand!

People who want to receive notifications of missing persons in your area are invited to register for SARA on this website. Supported Law Enforcement agencies will send time-sensitive notifications directly to your phone or computer.

How It Works

1 Sign up here or Sign up with Facebook.

2 Receive a single opt-in welcome message. Reply YES to continue to receive SMS text messages. You may opt out at any point by sending STOP to 123456. Send HELP to 123456 for information. No charge but Message and Data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.

3 Start helping with the search from right where you are. Just being alert on the roads, at the store, and in your own neighborhood will help. You never know when you will see something that makes the difference.

Become a first responder

First responders are approved in advance of an official search and may receive priority alerts. If you opt in as a first responder, your information will be used for a background check. If an official search is organized in a specific area, you will be notified of the place and time. Then you may be pre-approved to search and can skip the lines at the search headquarters.

SIGN UP USING YOUR PHONE. Simply text any zip code to 123456 and receive real-time alerts and advisories directly from your local police department and other local agencies. There is no charge for registering, but standard text messaging rates associated with your mobile phone service will apply. You can opt out at any time by texting "STOP" to 123456. If you're texting a zip code to 123456 and using SARA for the first time, you can retrieve a temporary password to complete the registration process here. Completing the registration process will allow you to fully customize how messages reach you - whether by mobile phone, email, or over the web at www.SARA.com - you decide!
